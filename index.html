Policies

Please read carefully before booking

Booking Policy

📅 Appointments

  • No extra guests or children allowed — space is limited.
  • Book at least 24 hours beforehand.
  • Arrive with bare nails unless you've booked a soak-off.

💳 Payment

  • A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking.
  • Remaining balance is due on the day of your appointment.
  • Cash or instant EFT accepted.

❌ Cancellation & Reschedule

  • Notify me at least 24 hours in advance to reschedule or cancel.
  • Deposits are non-refundable, but with 24+ hours notice can transfer to a new date.
  • Same-day cancellations or no-shows forfeit the deposit completely.

⏰ Late Policy

  • 15-minute grace period allowed.
  • After 15 minutes, a late fee of R50 applies.
  • After 20 minutes, appointment is cancelled.

Refund / Repair Policy

All services are non-refundable once completed. Inspect your nails before leaving — concerns must be raised immediately.

🔧 Repairs

  • Free repairs within 48 hours for lifting or breakage from application issues.
  • In-studio only — no house calls.
  • Contact within 48 hours with a clear photo.
  • Repairs after 48 hours are charged as a fix.
  • Subject to availability — not guaranteed same-day.

🚫 No Refunds or Repairs If:

  • Nails are bitten, picked, ripped, pulled or soaked off.
  • Damage caused by aftercare, lifestyle, or accidents.
  • Client cannot return in-studio for repairs.
  • Another nail tech has worked on the set.
  • Aftercare instructions were not followed.

💅 Retention Disclaimer

  • Retention varies per client — nail chemistry, lifestyle, length, and shape all play a role.
  • Long shapes (stiletto/coffin) require extra care.
