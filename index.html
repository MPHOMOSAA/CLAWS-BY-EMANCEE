Policies

Please read carefully before booking

Booking Policy

📅 Appointments

  • No extra guests or children allowed — space is limited.
  • Book your appointment at least 24 hours beforehand.
  • Please arrive with bare nails unless you've booked a soak-off.

💳 Payment

  • A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking.
  • The remaining balance is due on the day of your appointment.
  • I accept cash or instant EFT.

❌ Cancellation & Reschedule

  • To reschedule or cancel, please notify me at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Deposits are non-refundable, but with 24+ hours notice they can be transferred to a new date.
  • Same-day cancellations or no-shows will forfeit the deposit completely.

⏰ Late Policy

  • A 15-minute grace period is allowed.
  • After 15 minutes, a late fee of R50 will be added.
  • After 20 minutes, your appointment will be cancelled.

Refund / Repair Policy

All services are non-refundable once completed. Before leaving, please inspect your nails — any concerns must be raised immediately.

🔧 Repairs

  • Nail repairs are free within 48 hours for lifting or breakage caused by application issues.
  • Repairs can only be done in-studio — no house calls.
  • Clients must contact me within 48 hours with a clear photo.
  • Repairs outside 48 hours will be charged as a fix.
  • Repairs are done at my availability and are not guaranteed same-day.

🚫 No Refunds or Repairs If:

  • Nails are bitten, picked, ripped, pulled or soaked off.
  • Damage is caused by aftercare, lifestyle, or accidents.
  • Clients cannot return in-studio for repairs.
  • Another nail tech works on the set.
  • Aftercare instructions are not followed.

💅 Retention Disclaimer

  • Nail retention varies per client due to nail chemistry, lifestyle, length, and shape.
  • Long shapes (stiletto/coffin) require extra care.
